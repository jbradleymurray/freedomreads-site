Title: Craig L. Gore

----

Position: Communications Associate

----

Bio: Craig L. Gore is a Communications Associate at Freedom Reads and he also works in the Library Division. He was born and raised in New Haven and is the founder and executive director of *The Critical X-Change*, a creative and performing arts program that empowers young people through self-determination and creative self-expression. He believes in the transformative power of the arts as a tool for building confidence, leadership, and community.  As a returning citizen who spent nearly half his life incarcerated, he has a deep understanding of the importance of literacy and reading for personal growth for those behind prison walls. He is honored to bring his story-telling skills, his work ethic, and his passion to help those still in prison to Freedom Reads.
