**Introduction**

At Freedom Reads, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

**Third-Party Services**

Our website uses the following third-party services to enhance user experience, process transactions, and analyze site performance:

Google Analytics: Collects anonymized data, including IP addresses, browsing behavior, and site usage. This data helps us improve our website. For more information, see (link: https://policies.google.com/privacy text: Google's Privacy Policy).

Neon One: Processes donations and transactions. For more information, see (link: https://neonone.com/privacypolicy/ text: Neon One’s Privacy Policy).

Mapbox: Provides geolocation and mapping services. For more information, see (link: https://www.mapbox.com/legal/privacy text: Mapbox’s Privacy Policy).

YouTube and Spotify: These services may collect user preferences and behavioral data when you play embedded media. For more information, see (link: https://www.youtube.com/howyoutubeworks/our-commitments/protecting-user-data/ text: YouTube's Privacy Policy) and (link: https://www.spotify.com/us/legal/privacy-policy/ text: Spotify’s Privacy Policy). 

**How We Use Your Information**

We use the information collected solely to process transactions, improve our website, and communicate with you regarding your transactions. We do not share your data with third parties.

**Data Storage and Security**

Your data is stored securely on our web host’s servers. We have taken measures to protect your information, though no online data transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

**Changes to this Policy**

Freedom Reads may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on our website. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
